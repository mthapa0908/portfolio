My research focuses on parasitology with an emphasis on microbiology, exploring the interactions between parasitic organisms and their hosts. I am particularly interested in gastrointestinal parasites in wildlife, such as Blackbucks and Greater One-Horned Rhinos, and their ecological impacts. Additionally, I study intestinal helminths in domestic animals and humans, aiming to enhance understanding and improve management strategies for parasitic diseases.
